The Cat

Kitty

01

Feed 1 scoop every morning. She might not eat all of the old food, but throw out whatever is left and refill.

02

In the morning, throw out yesterday's water and refill.

03

Catbox is in the office closet. Clean it each day — she will pee outside the box if it isn't clean (sorry, it's a pain). There's a little bin next to it to make it easy. With this litter, just "stir" the pee and it dissolves, so don't worry that it doesn't scoop out.

04

As long as it is closed before sunset (because of raccoons and other cats), you can leave the porch door open for her to go in and out even if you leave the house for a few hours midday.

05

If you're hanging out, feel free to throw some treats for her.

Kitchen

Coffee, Booze & Food

Coffee

I have a coffee maker and grinder in the kitchen. Pretty standard — I recommend filling 30–40% of the basket with grounds for 6–8 cups of water in the pot.

Bar & Food

I have a stocked bar — please feel free to use any bottles displayed on the shelves. Help yourself to whatever food you find in the house and make yourself at home. Everything besides the large knives is fine in the dishwasher.

Please feel free to use the flattop outside to cook the diner breakfast of your dreams. It's great for pancakes, hashbrowns, eggs, fish — anything fun.

Other Kitchen Notes

There is a rice maker and a toaster underneath the bar.

House Notes

Other Details

Plants — I have plants throughout the house. If you're so inclined, I'd love if you could check to see if any look like they need water. Even just a cup here and there is nice. The outside plants are all on drip, so no worries there.

Mail — If you can, grab the mail and just bring it inside.

Laundry — There is laundry in the downstairs storage that you can use — same door code.

Sheets — in the left-side bedroom closet.

Trash cans — Don't worry about putting out the big ones.

Staying cool — Playing with opening and closing the windows should create a nice draft through the house. There is AC you can use in the evenings, but I also recommend dropping the blinds on the hallway windows during the afternoon sun.
